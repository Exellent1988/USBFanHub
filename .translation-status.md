## Translation Guidelines

1. **Technical terms:** Keep English (PWM, GPIO, USB HID, etc.)
2. **File paths:** Keep as-is
3. **Code examples:** Commands stay the same, comments translate
4. **Variable names:** Keep English
5. **Consistency:** Use consistent terminology across all files
